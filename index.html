About

Professional Summary

I have graduated with a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from IUBAT with a CGPA of 3.63/4.00, but the CS degree is only half the story. For the last four years, alongside coursework and internships, I have been tutoring HSC students through ICT and board exams, mentoring juniors through IUBAT's hardest courses, and running my own Math and English sessions at home — which is a roundabout way of saying I've spent as much time learning how to explain things clearly as I have learning how to build them.

On the engineering side, I've worked across Brain Station 23 and Sentinel Technologies LTD., building front-end interfaces with Angular and React. I've also been a published researcher with Dreamers Lab — three papers on sustainable construction materials and nanomaterials — and a certified mentor. I was General Secretary and Academic Wing Manager of the IUBAT IT Society, which meant running events and keeping a lot of very different people pointed in the same direction.
